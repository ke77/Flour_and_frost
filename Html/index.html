OUR STORY

In a modest kitchen, Michael Davis' passion for baking ignited a dream. With unwavering dedication, he founded a bakery, perfecting recipes, one pastry at a time. Word of mouth spread through delight, and soon, the bakery blossomed. Today, Flour&Frost stands as a beloved institution, renowned for artisanal creations, uniting the community in the shared joy of sweet indulgence and lasting memories.
